Contents of a single hive:

1 Queen Bee
100 - 500 male drones
20,000 - 80,000 Female Worker Bees
Eggs
Larvae
Pupae




Roles:
Queen - lays up to 2000 fertilized eggs per day,

Workers - most populous, female, cannot produce eggs. Use barbed stingers to defend the colony, results in death. Forage for pollen and nectar, tend to the queens and drones, feed larvae, ventilate the hive, defend the hive, live for six weeks.

Drones - have only one job - fertilize the queen + queens from other colonies. Drones mate outdoors usually in midair. They usually die first when food becomes limited. Dies after successful mating.
